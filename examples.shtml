Super-resolution

To compare upsampling methods, open the an image in two or more browser tabs that has been upsampled using different methods. Then switch between tabs and note the change in image quality. Hover the mouse over each thumbnail to determine the upsampling method.

Many browsers will open a new tab by control-clicking or right-clicking (Windows, Linux) or by command-clicking (Mac).
